## Content template (each review/comparison)

1. **Title:** [Primary keyword] 2026 (or "X vs Y 2026").  
2. **Meta description:** 150–160 chars, include primary keyword + value prop.  
3. **Intro:** 1–2 short paragraphs — problem + what we did (tested X tools, criteria).  
4. **Methodology callout:** "We prioritize integration maturity and AI ROI; see [How we test]."  
5. **Criteria:** 3–5 bullets (e.g. Integration, AI features, Price, Support, Security).  
6. **Top picks:** For each tool: 2–3 sentences, pros/cons, "Best for X."  
7. **Comparison table:** Rows = criteria, columns = tools.  
8. **Verdict:** 1–2 paragraphs; clear "Our pick" (primary CTA) and "Best value" / "Runner-up" (secondary CTA) to capture more clicks.  
9. **CTAs:** Primary and secondary with affiliate links; disclosure (e.g. "We may earn a commission if you use our links – at no extra cost to you").  
10. **Internal links:** To category hub, methodology, related comparison.  
11. **Footer:** Short affiliate disclosure if not global.
