Your audience consists of business owners, IT buyers, and developers searching for software. Their biggest pains right now:
*   **Tool Fatigue & Bloat:** Paying for 10 different tools that don't integrate well.
*   **"Is the AI actually useful?"** Fatigue from marketing hype; they want to know if AI will actually provide ROI (e.g., Cursor Composer vs standard Copilot).

*   **Compliance Pressures:** New data laws and remote work permanent shifts mean B2B teams are spending heavily on enterprise password managers and business VPNs.
