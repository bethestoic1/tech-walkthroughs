About Tech Walkthroughs

I started Tech Walkthroughs because I was exhausted by fake software reviews. Every "best software" list looks exactly the same—written by people who have never actually logged into a CRM or configured a zero-trust network.

I am a former Director of IT and RevOps who spent the last decade building real technology stacks for scaling companies. Now, I spend 100+ hours stress-testing CRM platforms, cybersecurity tools, and AI coding assistants so you don't have to waste your budget.

Why trust this site?

We don't just read the marketing copy. We actually set up the pipelines. We run the API payload tests. We configure the SAML SSO integrations. We focus strictly on integration maturity (how well the tool talks to your existing stack) and actual ROI (will this save your team time, or just add complexity?).

What we cover

We evaluate business software across three core pillars: Marketing & CRM, Cybersecurity, and AI Coding. Every comparison follows a rigorous, transparent methodology. We don't hand out "Editor's Choice" awards lightly.

The Business Model

Tech Walkthroughs is a reader-supported business. We earn commissions when you sign up through our links, but that never influences our testing results or rankings. If a tool has terrible customer support, we will tell you. You can read our full disclosure here.
