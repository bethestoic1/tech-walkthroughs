In 2025 NordVPN added post-quantum encryption to NordLynx — future-proofing against quantum decryption attacks with no measurable speed penalty. In our 200+ speed tests across servers in the US, UK, EU, and Asia-Pacific:

  • Average speed retention on NordLynx: 82% of baseline speed
  • ExpressVPN Lightway for comparison: 85% of baseline speed
  • Surfshark WireGuard: 80% of baseline speed

The 3% gap between NordVPN and ExpressVPN is imperceptible in real usage. On a 100Mbps connection: NordVPN delivers ~82Mbps, ExpressVPN ~85Mbps. For streaming, browsing, and video calls, this difference doesn't matter. The price difference - $3.99/month vs $8.32/month - absolutely does.

Security - industry-leading depth

Proven no-logs policy

NordVPN's no-logs policy has been independently audited five times: PricewaterhouseCoopers (2018, 2020) and Deloitte (2022, 2023, 2024). Threat Protection - the standout feature

Threat Protection Pro blocks ads, trackers, and malicious domains — and unlike basic DNS filtering, it works even when the VPN tunnel is off. It operates at the URL and JavaScript level (not just DNS), with machine-learning-based phishing detection. In our testing, it blocked 98.7% of known ad domains and eliminated 100% of tracker pings from the test set. This is a genuinely useful security feature that competitors don't offer at this quality level.

Double VPN and Onion Over VPN

For high-risk situations (journalists in restrictive countries, activists, whistleblowers), NordVPN's Double VPN routes traffic through two VPN servers. Onion Over VPN routes through the Tor network. Both features are available on standard NordVPN plans with no additional cost - no other major consumer VPN includes both.

Zero-knowledge DNS and RAM servers

NordVPN operates its own zero-knowledge DNS servers (no third-party DNS logging) and uses RAM-only servers that wipe clean on reboot. Physical server seizure produces no recoverable user data.

FAQ

Has NordVPN ever been hacked?

In 2018, a single NordVPN server in Finland (one of thousands) was accessed without authorization through a vulnerability at a third-party data center. No user credentials, activity logs, or private keys were exposed — because NordVPN's no-logs architecture means there was nothing stored to compromise. NordVPN terminated the data center contract, accelerated the move to RAM-only servers, and commissioned independent security audits. The incident validated the no-logs model: even with server access, attackers obtained no user data.

Is NordVPN good for torrenting?

Yes - NordVPN supports P2P traffic on designated servers and includes a kill switch that cuts internet access if the VPN drops. The no-logs policy means your torrenting activity is not stored. Use P2P-optimized servers for best speeds. NordVPN's Threat Protection also blocks malicious domains common in torrent ecosystems.
