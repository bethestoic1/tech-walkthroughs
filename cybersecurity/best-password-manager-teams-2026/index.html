Best Password Manager for Teams 2026

We tested team password managers for security, sharing, and admin controls. See our methodology.

Teams need shared vaults, role-based access, and recovery without sacrificing security. We compared the leading business password managers for 2026—security model, sharing, compliance, and ease of rollout.

How we evaluated

  • Security & encryption — Zero-knowledge, breach monitoring, and key management.
  • Sharing & vaults — Shared collections, permissions, and onboarding.
  • Admin & compliance — Policies, audit logs, and SSO.
  • Value — Price per seat and what’s included.
