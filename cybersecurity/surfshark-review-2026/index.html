Surfshark Review 2026: Unlimited Devices and the Lowest Price - But Is It Enough?

We tested Surfshark Starter, One, and One+ plans across 30 server locations with 150+ speed tests over 6 weeks. See our methodology.

Bottom Line: Great for large households, NordVPN wins for everyone else

Rating: 4.2/5. Surfshark's two standout features are unlimited simultaneous connections (every device in your household on one plan) and the lowest 2-year price of any major VPN at $2.49/month. For families or households with 10+ devices, these make Surfshark a compelling choice. For individuals and small teams where device count isn't the issue, NordVPN at $3.99/month delivers better speed, more servers, and a wider feature set for an extra $1.50/month.

What Surfshark does best

Unlimited simultaneous connections

Surfshark is the only major VPN with genuinely unlimited device connections on one plan. NordVPN allows 10, ExpressVPN allows 8. For a household with gaming consoles, smart TVs, phones, laptops, and tablets all wanting VPN coverage simultaneously, Surfshark's unlimited policy is a decisive advantage. At $2.49/month for the entire household, no other VPN comes close on per-device cost.

Price - cheapest major VPN

Surfshark's 2-year Starter plan at $2.49/month is the lowest price point of any reputable VPN. The catch: after the promotional 2-year period, renewal rates jump significantly ($3.99+ per month). Factor in the renewal rate when comparing long-term costs - NordVPN's renewal pricing is more predictable.

CleanWeb - solid ad and tracker blocking

Surfshark's CleanWeb feature blocks ads, trackers, and malicious sites at the DNS level, similar to NordVPN's Threat Protection. In our testing, CleanWeb blocked 96% of known ad domains vs NordVPN Threat Protection's 98.7%. Both are effective. Surfshark's CleanWeb is available on all plans including Starter - NordVPN's Threat Protection requires the Plus plan.

MultiHop (double VPN)

Like NordVPN's Double VPN, Surfshark's MultiHop routes traffic through two VPN servers for enhanced anonymity. Available on all plans. For high-risk use cases that benefit from layered VPN routing, both NordVPN and Surfshark support this - ExpressVPN does not.

Where Surfshark falls short

Speed - trails NordVPN slightly

In our testing, Surfshark's WireGuard protocol delivered 80% speed retention versus NordVPN's 82%. The difference is small but consistent across test locations. At peak times on popular servers, NordVPN's larger server network (6,400+ vs 3,200+) also means less congestion. For daily use the gap won't matter; for heavy bandwidth users, NordVPN delivers more headroom.

Streaming - weakest of the three

Surfshark unblocked Netflix in major regions 93% of the time versus NordVPN's 97% and ExpressVPN's 100%. BBC iPlayer (notoriously aggressive against VPNs) had an 89% success rate with Surfshark vs 96% with NordVPN. For streaming-focused users, Surfshark is the weakest of the three options.

Server network depth

3,200 servers across 100 countries is strong - but NordVPN's 6,400+ servers means nearly double the density in most regions. In congested periods, NordVPN users find uncongested servers more easily. Surfshark's virtual servers in some locations also produce slightly higher latency than NordVPN's physical servers in the same regions.

FAQ

Is Surfshark really unlimited devices?

Yes - Surfshark's single plan covers unlimited simultaneous connections with no sub-accounts or device profiles required. You can run Surfshark on your phone, laptop, TV, gaming console, partner's phone, and family tablet all at the same time on one subscription. No other major VPN offers this.

Is Surfshark safe?

Yes. Surfshark's no-logs policy was audited by Deloitte in 2023. The WireGuard and OpenVPN protocols are industry-standard and the company's jurisdiction (Netherlands) is outside the 5/9/14 Eyes intelligence sharing agreements for practical purposes. Surfshark is a legitimate, safe VPN provider.

Final Verdict

Rating: 4.2/5. Surfshark is the right choice for large households where unlimited devices and lowest total cost matter most. For most individuals and small teams, NordVPN at $3.99/month is the better overall value - faster, more servers, better streaming unblocking, and Threat Protection as a bonus.
