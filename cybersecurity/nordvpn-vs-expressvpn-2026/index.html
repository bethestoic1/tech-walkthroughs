NordVPN vs ExpressVPN 2026

NordVPN and ExpressVPN are two of the most searched VPNs. We tested both for speed, server coverage, no-logs policies, and business-friendly features to give you a clear head-to-head for 2026.

How we compared

  • Speed & reliability — Throughput and consistency across regions.
  • No-logs & trust — Audits, jurisdiction, and transparency.
  • Business features — Teams, central billing, and deployment.
  • Value — Price and what you get (devices, support).

NordVPN

Strong speeds, audited no-logs policy, and a clear business offering (NordVPN Enterprise). Good server coverage and features for teams. Our pick for most teams that want one trusted vendor for business VPN.

ExpressVPN

Historically strong on speed and ease of use. No-logs policy and trust signals; business offering has evolved. Often priced at a premium. Good choice if you prioritize simplicity and are willing to pay more.
