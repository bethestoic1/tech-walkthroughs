HubSpot vs Monday 2026

We compared HubSpot and Monday.com on integration maturity, AI, and value. See our methodology.

HubSpot and Monday.com are two of the most searched CRM and work-management tools. We ran both through the same criteria—integrations, AI features, pricing, and real workflows—to give you a clear head-to-head for 2026.

How we compared

  • Integration maturity — How well each plugs into your stack (email, calendar, apps).
  • AI features — What the AI actually does (drafts, automation, insights) and whether it’s worth the cost.
  • Pricing & value — Entry cost, scaling, and what you get at each tier.
  • Use case fit — Pure CRM vs CRM + projects/ops.

HubSpot

Stronger as a full-funnel CRM: marketing, sales, and service in one platform. AI is built into content, lead scoring, and follow-ups. Best if you want an all-in-one growth stack and are willing to invest. Cost scales with contacts and features.

Monday.com

Better as a flexible work OS that can act as CRM: boards, automations, and apps. Often better value at lower seat counts and for teams that also need project/ops. Steeper learning curve for “classic” sales pipeline use.
